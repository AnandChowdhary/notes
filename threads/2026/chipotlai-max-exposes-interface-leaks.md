The joke is funny because it makes the abstraction leak. If the agent loop speaks OpenAI-compatible HTTP, the model behind it can be Claude, a local model, or apparently a burrito support bot that learned LeetCode. Not a product recommendation. Very much a 'look at what standardized interfaces do to the world' recommendation. https://github.com/cyberpapiii/chipotlai-max
